About

Go Pound Salt was three-piece Noise Pop, Pop Punk, Rock band from San Francisco that existed from '95-'97. While we broke up ages ago, we spent the last few years releasing some recordings found way after the fact.

Releases

Branded by Go Pound Salt

Coming soon!

"Branded" is three tracks recorded in '96 during the same sessions of the self-titled cd. They were originally released as a 7-inch but masters and remaining vinyl were lost ages ago.

In 2022, the original tracks were transferred to digital, re-mixed, master and will be released soon.

Last Demo by Go Pound Salt

Recorded August 17, 1997 at Stout Recording by Randy Burk in San Francisco, CA.

This was the last set of recordings we did before we broke up later that year. Originally released on cassette.

Available now on Bandcamp.

The Lost Tapes by Go Pound Salt

Eight songs recorded sometime in 1996/97 at Downtown Rehearsal in San Francisco, CA.

In 2017, the unfinished tapes were found, digitized, new guitar and vocal parts recorded.

Available now on Bandcamp.

Self-titled by Go Pound Salt

Recorded 1996 at Russian Hill Recording in San Francisco, CA.

Our only full-length cd.

Available now on Bandcamp.
