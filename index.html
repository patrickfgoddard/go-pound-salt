About

Go Pound Salt was a Noise Pop, Pop Punk, Rock band from San Francisco that existed from '95-'97. We are back with a soon-to-be-released batch of newly discover songs.

Out Now!

The Lost Tapes is a collection of eight never-before-released songs by Go Pound Salt. These songs were recorded in 1997, stuck in a box and forgotten about, until 20 years later in 2017. The audio was rescued using modern technology with and enhanced with new guitar and vocal tracks.

The Lost Tapes documents Go Pound Salt at a creative-high, an eclective mix of pop punk, math rock and cow punk.

Available now on Bandcamp.
